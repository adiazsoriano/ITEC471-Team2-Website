E-Book Store

The Project

Our project consists of building a microservices-based e-commerce platform that allows users to create profile, browse products, add them to the cart, and place orders. The platform will consist of several microservices, each responsible for a specific functionality. Together, all will come together to create a pristine electronic bookstore for all to use. It is our mission at LATA to deliver the best for our users.
